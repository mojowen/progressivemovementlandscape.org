About

An unprecedented number of new effort and organizations have bloomed in response to the election of Donald Trump, adding to the robust landscape of progressive movement. Even for those who have been engaged for years, mapping and situating the various organizations, platforms, and networks emerging has been a challenge.

The Progressive Movement Landscape is an effort to help activists, funders, and organizations identify entities operating within the progressive movement. The Progressive Movement Landscape is an aggregate of publicly available data collected by volunteers. This information is sortable, filterable, and downloadable for anyoneâ€™s use. It is a living document and we welcome feedback to make corrections, additions, subtractions to maintain its accuracy.

We hope this information helps you work with greater coordination, efficiency, and support (people, money and tools).
